Cosmic Happenings

Cosmic Happenings are random events that affect the entire map and the players. Some Cosmic Happenings give boosts to all players, some to a few players and some are more negative. The Cosmic Happenings cannot be predicted. They won’t happen before turn 3. Every turn after that there is a small chance on Cosmic happening that increases every turn.
