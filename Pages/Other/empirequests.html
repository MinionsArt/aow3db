Empire Quests

Empire Quests are global quests that you compete to complete with other players


Heroic Skill

Goal: Be the first to have a Hero or Leader reach level 15

Reward: The hero gains 5 bonus Skill Points.

Paragon

Goal: Be the first to build or summon a Tier IV unit

Reward: This unit gains a permanent bonus.
Unit gains
Resurgence
, +10 Hitpoints, +1 Defense, and +1 Resistence

Centurion

Goal: Be the first to have 100 units

Reward: All of your units level up instantly.

Multiculture

Goal: Be the first player with cities of 5 different races

Reward: A group of units from those races.

Prime Good

Goal: Be the first to achieve Pure Good alignment

Reward: A group of Dedicated to Good units.

Prime Evil

Goal: Be the first to achieve Pure Evil alignment

Reward: A group of Dedicated to Evil units.

Eternal City

Goal: Be the first to own a Metropolis.

Reward: The City receives a monument that grants +5 Production, +5 Gold , +5 Mana and +5 Knowledge.

Sage

Goal: Be the first to research a tier VI Skill.

Reward: You gain 2 Secret Skills

All Knowing

Goal: Be the first to research all skills.

Reward: You receive 2 new Specialisations chosen at random

Heptatopia

Goal: Be the first to acquire 7 cities.

Reward: You receive a +100 Empire Happiness Necromancer players will receive a 5% bonus to city income instead.

Imperial Residence

Goal: Be the first to construct a Grand Palace.

Reward: Your Grand Palace will grant +10 extra Casting Points and +100 City Happiness

Master Smith

Goal: Be the first to construct an Arcane Item Forge.

Reward: You will receive a powerful magical item in your city
