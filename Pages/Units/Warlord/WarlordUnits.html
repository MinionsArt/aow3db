Warlord

Because Warlords tend not to wield many offensive powers, it is tempting to believe that territories ruled by Warlords are easy to conquer. History has proven this to be foolishness.
Warlords lead the masses by the heart. They heal, persuade, minister and convert. Their zealots are fiercely loyal, convinced to the core that any action they perform will earn them a greater reward in the afterlife. Warlords bestow power upon their most faithful from two theological sources: the Mandate of Heaven or the terror of the Underworld.
